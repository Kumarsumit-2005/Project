Library

The library at CUTM serves as a hub of knowledge, exploration, and academic growth. It offers a diverse collection of resources including books, journals, and digital materials to support research, learning, and personal enrichment. With comfortable study spaces and state-of-the-art facilities, it provides an ideal environment for students to delve into their studies and pursue intellectual endeavors. The library staff are dedicated professionals who assist students in navigating resources, conducting research, and expanding their information literacy skills. Beyond books and databases, the library hosts workshops, seminars, and events that foster a culture of learning and discovery. It's not just a place to find information; it's a community where ideas are exchanged, collaborations are formed, and minds are inspired. At CUTM, the library is more than a collection of books; it's a gateway to endless possibilities and academic excellence. Come explore, learn, and grow with us at the heart of knowledge and innovation.

At CUTM, our garden is a thriving ecosystem teeming with life and diversity. Buzzing bees flit from flower to flower, pollinating our fruit trees and ensuring a bountiful harvest. Among the lush greenery, fruit trees stand tall, offering a variety of delicious treats for our community to enjoy. From crisp apples to juicy oranges, there's always something fresh and flavorful to pick straight from the branches. But our garden isn't just about fruit trees; it's a sanctuary for vibrant flowers that paint the landscape with bursts of color and fragrance. As the petals sway gently in the breeze, they attract not only bees but also butterflies, creating a mesmerizing display of nature's beauty. In addition to fruits and flowers, our garden boasts a thriving vegetable patch where a variety of greens flourish. From crunchy cucumbers to plump tomatoes, our organic vegetables are cultivated with care and sustainably harvested to nourish both body and soul. Moreover, our garden is home to a tranquil fishpond, where colorful koi glide gracefully beneath the water's surface. The gentle ripple of the pond and the soothing presence of the fish provide a sense of serenity and connection to the natural world. At CUTM, our garden isn't just a place of growth; it's a vibrant ecosystem where bees, fruits, flowers, vegetables, and fish coexist harmoniously, enriching our lives and fostering a deeper appreciation for the wonders of nature.
