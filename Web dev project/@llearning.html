Dr. Sujata Chakravarty
Dean,
SoET,
BBSR

Dr. Sujata Chakravarty, Senior Member of IEEE, is currently working as Dean SoET, Professor CSE and Coordinator DSML Research Centre, CUTM, Odisha. There are about 120 Publications, eight patents published, one patent granted, one book and 25 book chapters to her credit. Recently three of her articles have been recognized by WHO and are being listed on the WHO website. Under her guidance about 150 BTech, 20 MTech and three PhD scholars got awarded.


Currently ten PhD scholars are working in areas like Financial Engineering, Bio-medical engineering, Smart Agriculture, Intrusion Detection, Image Processing, Hyperspectral and Multispectral Imaging. She has been awarded Jhansi Rani Laxmibai Prativa Puraskar for Technical Education and Research on 8th March 2018, Abdul Kalam Chair Professor Award on 5th September 2021 in Provost Research Conclave, Medal of Honour and Certificate of Excellence on 15th August 2022.

Dr. Sangram Keshari Swain is a Ph.D. in CSE having 15 years of teaching experience. He also holds B Tech (IT), M Tech (IT), MBA (HR), LLB & PGDRD degrees. He is an Experienced Teacher & Passionate Researcher.


He is a strong education professional having multidimensional approaches like Engineering, Technology, Management and Law. He is Skilled in Teaching, Research, Data Analysis, Social Services, Students’ Welfare, and Higher Education. Social Responsibility Initiative is his Passion always. His research interests and activities include Data Mining, Data Analytics, Big Data, AI, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, IoT, Waste Management and Social Development. He is has published 02 Patents, 02 Copyrights, more than 30 Technical papers in different Indexed Journals. He has Presented more than 10 Technical papers in different National & International conferences.
